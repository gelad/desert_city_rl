Arrow keys and Numpad 1-4, 6-9 - movement, target selection, etc.

Numpad 5 - wait for 1 turn (time equivalent to 1 attack or 1 step).
l - look around
c - close door (choose direction with movement keys)
s - smash (choose direction with movement keys)
g - pick up item from tile you're standing on
i - inventory menu
e - equipped items menu
w - wield (equip) item
o - take off equipped item
d - drop item from inventory
u - use item from inventory
t - throw item in hands
f - shoot ranged weapon
r - reload ranged weapon
U - unload ranged weapon
